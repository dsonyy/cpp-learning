You don't need type all commands. You can also type first char of the word. (except for the names of items and ores - see Minerals and items header). Basic: GO - go ahead BACK - go back NOW - show last menu CLOSE - close the active menu OPEN what - open what Minerals: MINE what - mine what DIG - mine 1B of ore (use that after mine what) DIG ALL - mine all B of ore (use that after mine what) NEED - list of minerals needed to escape current place RECAST what - recast what (furnance menu) Items: USE what - use what BAG - open your main bag INV - open your inventory TAKE what - take what TAKE ALL - take all items DROP what - drop what DROP ALL - drop all items (not recommended) Npcs n mobs: TELL - tell with npcs NPC - list of meet npcs MOB - list of known mobs Battle: STATS - info about player HIT - hit the nearest mob/npc Other: HELP - command list EAT what - eat what YES/NO - yes/no ----------------------------------------------------------------- ..:::Minerals n items:::.. co - coal ir - iron ha - halite cp - copper st - stone S - sapphire E - emerald R - ruby T - topaz L - lapis lazuli g - gold s - silver d - diamond l - life crystal p - philosopher's stone ----------------------------------------------------------------- ..:::History of versions:::.. x.yyzz x - basic number, yy - creation day, zz - creation month 0.1007 - basic engine of game 0.1307 - add chests and some tiny shits 0.1407 - add some NPCs 0.1507 - new ores generator ----------------------------------------------------------------- ..:::Cheats:::..
